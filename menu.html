WASI: Print Menu

For this project, I had to create a print menu for a fictional restaurant. I came up with ‘Wasi”: A Peruvian restaurant and bistro that caters to a younger audience while keeping the same feel of traditional Peruvian restaurants.

Rather than creating a menu for an already established brand, I had to think about who the restaurant would cater to and envision the ambience in theory. Another difficulty I encountered is having the translate the colorful feel while keeping the menu legible.

Adobe InDesign was used to design and layout the menu's pages, incorporating text, images, and graphics to create an aesthetically pleasing and informative document.
