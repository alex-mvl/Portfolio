Nice to meet you!

Hi! My name is Alexandra Vega, a junior majoring in Integrated Design and Media at New York University. I am a proud first-generation child of immigrants from Peru who have passed down their culture and language to me. I have a strong sense of community because of how I was raised. Something that has always pushed me forward is wanting to see people who look like me excel in college and my field of study.

My focus lies in User Experience design and web development, where I love creatively solving difficult problems while also thinking about different perspectives. Proficient in Javascript, Python, HTML, C#, and tools like Unity, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, and Ableton Live, I am equipped to bring innovative ideas to life using these different platforms.

Beyond academics, I love discovering new and interesting hobbies. Gaming, baking, pottery, and crocheting are some of many. These activities not only help me relax but also refuel my creativity and problem-solving skills when I have a block. Let's connect and explore opportunities to collaborate creatively!
